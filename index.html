PRIME LOCATIONS

Nepal’s Wild Heartlands

Chitwan National Park

UNESCO World Heritage Site, spanning 952 km². Home to one-horned rhinos, Bengal tigers, leopards, sloth bears, and over 650 bird species. Enjoy serene canoe rides on Rapti River & Tharu cultural experiences.

Bardiya National Park

Known as "Tigerland" — 968 km² of dense sal forests, grasslands, and river corridors. Prime habitat for Royal Bengal Tigers, wild elephants, Gangetic dolphins and over 400 bird species.
